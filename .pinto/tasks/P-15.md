# **Summary**

Complete the archive workflow by listing and viewing archived PBIs and restoring them safely to the active backlog.

# **Acceptance Criteria**

- [ ] `pinto list --archived` lists archived PBIs, and `pinto show <id> --archived` displays an archived item's details.
- [ ] `pinto restore <id>` returns an archived PBI to the active task store without changing its content or ID.
- [ ] Normal list, board, and show operations continue to exclude archived PBIs unless the archive option is supplied.
- [ ] Restoration refuses an ID collision without overwriting either copy.
