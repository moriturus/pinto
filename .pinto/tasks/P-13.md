# **Summary**

Warn Scrum users when the points assigned to a Sprint exceed its configured capacity or the selected velocity comparison, without blocking the workflow.

# **Acceptance Criteria**

- [ ] `pinto sprint start` warns when the assigned point total exceeds the applicable capacity or velocity threshold.
- [ ] `pinto sprint add` provides the same warning when the assignment would exceed the applicable threshold.
- [ ] The warning is non-blocking and the requested valid operation still completes.
- [ ] No warning is emitted when the assigned work is within the applicable threshold or no comparison is configured.
