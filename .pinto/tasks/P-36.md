# **Summary**

Every file-backend operation reads and parses the complete task and archive set before acting, so command latency grows linearly with board size, including single-item reads. The fail-fast validation contract in `docs/stability.md` motivates this, but the latency curve on large boards has never been measured.

# **Acceptance Criteria**

- [ ] A reproducible benchmark measures `list`, `show`, `add`, and `move` on generated boards of at least 1,000 and 10,000 items.
- [ ] The results are recorded in `docs/benchmarks.md`.
- [ ] A design or stability note records whether single-item reads may bypass full-board parsing, or why the current contract stands.
