# **Summary**

Keep localized English errors and library display messages consistent without altering diagnostics supplied by external systems.

# **Acceptance Criteria**

- [ ] The intended relationship between localized English messages and `Error::to_string()` is defined for every public error.
- [ ] Representative errors are tested for matching meaning and actionable recovery guidance.
- [ ] Structured error information remains available until the localization boundary where possible.
- [ ] OS, Git, TOML, and other external diagnostics retain their original text.
